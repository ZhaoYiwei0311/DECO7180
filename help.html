How it works

Navigate your way to a safer property choice

🗺️ RiskMap

Harness the power of our RiskMap feature 🗺️ to ascertain the safety index of your preferred area 🏚️. This interactive tool allows users to filter and view specific disasters in designated regions, providing a comprehensive perspective on potential risks ⭐️.

📊 Ranking

Explore specific details about different suburbs in Brisbane. This feature provides you with an overview of the median house prices 💰, area descriptions, and basic rental and sales price information for each suburb 🌃. Get a better understanding of your neighbourhood or compare different ones by sorting 🧷 and searching 🔍 different areas!

📈 Analysing

Discover insights into specific areas with our in-depth data exploration 📑 feature. Analyze charts depicting flood 🌊, wildfire 🔥, and crime events 👮 over the past five years, and compare these figures to Brisbane's averages for a comprehensive perspective on safety conditions ⭐️.

Discover your ideal property in Brisbane using our platform. Delve into detailed profiles of available properties, deciphering their locations 📍 , safety indices, and crime rates. Leverage this feature to make data-driven, informed decisions for your next home 🏡.
